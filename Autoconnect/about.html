About AUTO CONNECT


Established in November of the previous year by founder Ronaldo Tarroza, AUTO CONNECT was born out of a vision to revolutionize the car service industry in the Philippines. With a mission to simplify the process for car owners in need of quality mechanics, AUTO CONNECT introduces a groundbreaking approach to automotive care through its Doorstep - Pickup & Drop service.

At AUTO CONNECT, we understand the challenges faced by car owners in finding reliable and skilled mechanics. Our founder, Ronaldo Tarroza, envisioned a service that not only brings convenience but also guarantees top-tier troubleshooting by deploying a team of highly skilled mechanics across the Philippines.



Here are our core values

Best Quality

AUTO CONNECT we are committed to providing the highest quality service to ensure the optimal performance and longevity of your vehicle. Our team of skilled technicians utilizes advanced technology and expertise to deliver top-notch maintenance and repairs, ensuring that every customer experiences the best in automotive care. Your satisfaction and the reliability of your vehicle are our priorities, and we strive to exceed expectations by consistently delivering excellence in car services.

Sustainable

At AUTO CONNECT, sustainability is at the forefront of our mission. We are dedicated to providing eco-friendly and sustainable car services that minimize environmental impact. From utilizing energy-efficient equipment to adopting eco-friendly practices in our operations, we are committed to reducing our carbon footprint. Our commitment extends to using environmentally responsible products and promoting recycling initiatives. By choosing our sustainable car services, you not only ensure the longevity and efficiency of your vehicle but also contribute to a cleaner and healthier planet for future generations.

Integrity

At AUTO CONNECT, we take pride in upholding the highest standards of integrity in every aspect of our operations. Our commitment to integrity means transparent communication, honest assessments, and fair pricing for all our customers. We believe in building trust through genuine and ethical practices, ensuring that every recommendation and service is in the best interest of our customers. With a focus on transparency, reliability, and accountability, we strive to foster long-term relationships based on integrity, making us your trusted partner for all your car service needs.
